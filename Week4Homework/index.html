Goats

Capra hircus

I'm not "kidding" around when I say, 'I love goats!'

Why do I love goats? I'll tell you why...

  1. I worked with them when I first started being a zookeeper.
    • I learned a lot from working with goats that helped me advance in my career and they'll always have a special place in my heart as the first animals I got to work with.
  2. They are diverse!
    • There are over 300 distinct breeds of goats. Some many different sizes, colors, and personalities!
  3. They make me laugh!
    • I can just look at them and they make me giggle. They have crazy eyes, fat bellies, and make weird noises. What's not to love?
